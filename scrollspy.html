메뉴1

A long way above the plants, at the top of Pig’s legs, there was a long tail. “I don’t believe I have ever seen a pig with such a long neck,” laughed Rosemary. “Have you ever seen a pig with such a long neck?” Rosemary asked Basil. “I think Pig’s front legs are longer than the back legs,” said Basil, ignoring the sisters. “Don’t be silly, why would a pig want longer front legs?” The sisters demanded. The plants were still talking about this, when a very long dribble landed in the flowerpot. Pig, the giraffe, lowered his long neck and thought, “I hope they taste as good as they look.” He wrote me a long letter. I wrote a long letter to her. No river in the world is longer than the Nile.
